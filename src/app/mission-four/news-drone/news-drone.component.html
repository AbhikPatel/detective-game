Drone Mayday
Pizza-Carrying Drone Crash in Lakewood

Residents of Lakewood County are considering themselves lucky after an autonomous drone fell from the sky, narrowly missing a senior questrian center.

The pointless aircraft, operated by local pizza entrepreneurs, Pie in the sky,was out for a piping hot delivery when witnesses say it all went wrong. "I heard what sounded like soft cheese caught in a propeller, and looked up," said shaken local mail carrier Ramesh Gandhi. "As it banked, one of the flaps on the pizza box opened up, and a hot stream of tomato sauce got stucked into the blades... the drone didn't stand a chance."

FAA official were on-site to access the drone wreckage while FDA agents arrived and promptly classified the pizza debris as "inedible."

Representatives for pie in the sky pizza released a statement saying:"We're profoundly sorry for the loss of the large sausage and mushroom pizza with extra bell peppers and any inconvenience it caused to our loyal customer of South Robb Street in Lakewood County. We're grateful that no one was harmed and will take the necessary steps to avoid future incidents."
